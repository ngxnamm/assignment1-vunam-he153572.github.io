What does it take to learn graphic design?






1. Get motivated

The path to pursuing a creative career is always fraught with thorns, so before you start studying Graphic Design, you should identify yourself with a clear source of motivation.






2. Having “qualities”

“Beautiful drawing” seems to be the most important quality in Graphic Design, but it is not. You will be surprised to know that in addition to hand-drawing, to learn Graphic Design you also need to have the following qualities:Love the pictures and colors, likes “ tinkering” with modern technology, never stop learning.
